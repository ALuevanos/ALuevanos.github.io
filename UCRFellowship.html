UCR DS-PATH Fellowship

A hands-on program that boosted my data science abilities through workshops, real-world projects, and career development resources.

Program Overview

I joined the UCR DS-PATH Fellowship to deepen my data science expertise in a collaborative, supportive setting. The program included practical workshops, team-based assignments, and networking events that helped me sharpen both my technical and professional skills.

Activities and Skills Acquired

This fellowship was a springboard for my professional and technical growth, providing opportunities to learn best practices and apply them in real-world contexts.

Workshops and Training

  • Explored R and Python fundamentals, emphasizing data structures, cleaning, and analysis.
  • Learned data visualization techniques to effectively communicate insights.
  • Gained foundational knowledge of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and its real-world applications.
  • Dived into deep learning fundamentals, working with neural networks and popular frameworks.
  • Mastered SQL queries and database management for efficient data retrieval and storage.

Project Development

  • Partnered with LA City on a data analytics project using data cleaning, analysis, and ArcGIS, transforming raw information into actionable insights and publishing them on the ELP website..
  • Presented our work in a scientific poster session, honing my communication and presentation skills.

Professional Development

  • Attended workshops on resume building and interview strategies, strengthening my professional presence.
  • Connected with academics and industry leaders, expanding my professional network.
  • Learned to present complex data science findings in a clear, reproducible manner.

Outcomes

  • Enhanced programming and data analysis skills in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment.
  • Refined my collaboration and presentation abilities while managing project timelines and deliverables.
  • Developed a deeper appreciation for cutting-edge data science research and its impact across industries.
